Virtual Tours




Waikato River and their Parks

Virtual walk of The Waikato River, New Zealand's longest at 425 kilometers. Journey through the War Memorial park, Parana park, Claudelands bridge, let the natural beauty of the parks by the river inspire and captivate you.








Hamilton Lake

Virtual walk of the Hamilton Lake, also known as Lake Rotoroa. Nestled in the heart of Hamilton, this serene spot features greenery scapes and diverse birdlife. Enjoy the stunning lakeside views and experience a perfect escape from the busyness in life.








Knighton Lake

Virtual Walk around Knighton Lake. See the tranquil beauty of Knighton Lake that is nestled within the University of Waikato campus. This serene lake offers a peaceful mood surrounded by greenery.
